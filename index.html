What is FiNIT?

FiNIT, the Finance Society of MANIT Bhopal, is a student-driven initiative dedicated to fostering financial literacy and awareness among the campus community. We aim to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and real-world financial practices through interactive workshops, seminars, and collaborative events. Our mission is to empower students with the skills and insights necessary to navigate the complex world of finance confidently.

Our Events

Wall Street Wizards

This exhilarating challenge is designed to test participants' financial acumen, strategic thinking, and market instincts. Participants are immersed in a dynamic environment that simulates real-world financial scenarios, requiring them to make quick decisions and adapt to changing market conditions

F-invest : The Flagship Event of FiNIT

This annual event is designed to immerse students in the world of finance through a series of engaging activities and competitions. Finvest features a variety of sub-events, including stock market simulations, investment challenges, and strategic games.
